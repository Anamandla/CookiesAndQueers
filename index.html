Cookies & Queers is a queer events collective rooted in Cape Town. We create safe, celebratory spaces where LGBTQIA+ folks can connect, play, and be fully themselves.

From intimate socials to dance floors and creative workshops — our events are tailored to our community, centring care, consent, and access.
